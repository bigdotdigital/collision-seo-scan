The Challenge

Werkheiser had the shop depth and production capacity, but search traffic was leaking to larger franchise names with weaker repair quality and weaker certification depth.

The objective was to turn certification and capability into booked collision work across Denver, with pages built for high-intent terms and cleaner conversion flow.

The Solution

We repositioned the site around OEM-certified capability (Subaru, Ford, GM and related makes), deployed Ford Transit and fleet/work-van landing pages, and shipped competitor-capture pages for high-value terms.

We also rebuilt page hierarchy for estimate starts, tightened trust/authority blocks, and improved Google Business profile performance so search visibility converted into better calls.
